## Mapping in Parallel Design（并行设计中的映射）  
  
**Mapping is the fourth and final stage of our parallel design process（映射是我们并行设计流程中的第四个也是最后一个阶段）. It involves specifying where each established task will be executed（它涉及指定每个已确定任务将在哪里执行）.**  
  
### Applicability（适用性）  
  
Mapping is not applicable for single-processor systems or systems with automated task scheduling（映射不适用于单处理器系统或具有自动任务调度的系统）. It becomes relevant in distributed systems or specialized hardware with multiple parallel processors（它在分布式系统或具有多个并行处理器的专用硬件中变得相关）.  
  
### Goals and strategies（目标与策略）  
  
The main goal of mapping is to minimize total execution time（映射的主要目标是最小化总执行时间）. Two key strategies are（有两个关键策略）:  
- Increasing concurrency: Place tasks capable of executing concurrently on different processors（提高并发性：将能并发执行的任务分配到不同的处理器上）.  
- Improving locality: Place frequently communicating tasks on the same processor to keep them close together（提高局部性：将频繁通信的任务放在同一个处理器上以保持它们的接近性）.  
  
### Challenges and considerations（挑战与考量）  
  
These strategies often conflict, requiring design trade-offs（这些策略经常冲突，需要进行设计权衡）.    
Dynamic workloads may necessitate periodic remapping using dynamic load-balancing techniques（动态工作负载可能需要通过动态负载均衡技术定期重新映射）.    
Mapping algorithm design depends heavily on program structure and hardware specifications（映射算法设计高度依赖于程序结构和硬件规格）.    
Various load-balancing algorithms utilize domain decomposition and agglomeration techniques（各种负载均衡算法利用域分解和聚合技术）.
